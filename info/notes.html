Nevsky: Implementation Notes

This implementation and the Nevsky materials on this site are the second edition rules.

Sequence of play changes for expediency:

  • One side does all of Feed, Pay, and Disband before next side.
  • Russians Feed, Pay, and Disband first after a Russian command card.
  • One side does both Wastage and Discard before next side.
  • Loser does all their Ending the Battle steps before the victor.
  • Crossbow hits are always applied before regular hits when mixed.
